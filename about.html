About Me


Brian Travelstead


Hello. My name is Brian Travelstead. I have recently returned to school in pursuit of a new career path. My career so far has been in the culinary arts and food service industry. I have worked in a variety of kitchens over the past 20 years, from fast food to fine dining. Cooking has always been a passion of mine, and I enjoy trying new foods and experimenting with new recipes in my free time (whatever that is). I hope to find a way to combine my experiences in the kitchen with my future career in software development.
